Automation isn't just hardware. It's the engineering that makes it work. Here is how we deploy the Cypher ecosystem in your facility.

01

Panel Fabrication

Off-Site Assembly

We don't build messy controls in the field. Every Cypher Edge-X1 controller is pre-mounted in a NEMA-rated enclosure at our shop. We install DIN rails, wire ducts, and terminal blocks, ensuring a clean, industrial-grade assembly that is easy to service for years to come.

  • Custom DIN Rail Mounts
  • High-Voltage Separation
  • Pre-Wired Transformers & Fuse Blocks
02

Custom Programming

Logic & Algorithms

We configure the Cypher OS specifically for your building's equipment. We set up the PID loops for precise temperature control, configure safety interlocks, and design the mobile-responsive floorplans. This isn't "out of the box"—it's tailored engineering.

  • Sequence of Operations (SOO) Setup
  • Graphics Generation
  • Database Configuration
03

Field Installation

On-Site Execution

Our technicians mount the panels and run the low-voltage communication networks (RS-485/Ethernet). We install sensors in strategic locations to ensure accurate data collection and terminate all field wiring to the Cypher I/O modules.

  • Panel Mounting
  • Sensor Placement
  • Communication Bus Wiring
04

Commissioning

Testing & Validation

The most critical step. We verify every single input and output point. We force the system into "Live Mode" and test safety shutdowns, economizer operation, and schedule overrides. We don't leave until the system is proven to work.

  • Point-to-Point Checkout
  • PID Loop Tuning
  • Customer Training
